Privacy Policy – Play Clock

Last updated: April 2026

Data collection

Play Clock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no internet access and does not communicate with any server.

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • VIBRATE – to vibrate the watch in the final seconds of the countdown.
  • WAKE_LOCK – to keep the screen on while the clock is running.

Neither permission is used to collect or share data.

Third-party services

The app uses no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising libraries.
